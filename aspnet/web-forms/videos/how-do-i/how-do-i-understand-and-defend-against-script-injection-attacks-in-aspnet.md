<a name="how-do-i-understand-and-defend-against-script-injection-attacks-in-aspnet"></a>Jak pochopit a braňte se proti Injektáži skriptu útoků v ASP.NET
====================
podle [Joe Stagner](https://github.com/JoeStagner)
